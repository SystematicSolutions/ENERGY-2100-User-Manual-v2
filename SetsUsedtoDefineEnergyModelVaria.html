Sets Used to Define Energy Model Variable

Sets represent the dimensions by which multi-dimensional arrays are delineated. Each set is defined in the model through .csv files Database\Sets\*.csv


The main sets defined in ENERGY 2100 are listed below with links to listings of their set elements, set descriptors and set keys.


Sets used to Dimension ENERGY 2100 Model Variable Arrays

Area

All areas represented in ENERGY 2100

Biofuel

Biofuels included in the Biofuel Module

Class

Consumer classes represented in the Electricity Module. Used to delineate electricity sales and loads.

CNArea

Subset of Area set containing only the Canada areas

Crude

Types of crude oil contained in Refinery Module

DACTech

Direct Air Capture technology types 

Day

Categories of times day (peak, average, minimum) used in the electricity dispatch

EC

Economic categories represented in the residential, commercial, industrial, and transportation demand sectors. Subset of ECC set.

ECC

Master list of all economic categories represented in the demand module

Enduse

Enduses simulated in the residential, commercial, industrial, and transportation demand sectors

ES

Energy Sectors represented in the energy market 

Feedstock

Types of feedstock energy used in the Biofuel Module

Fuel

Master list of detailed fuel types

FuelEP

Emission-producing fuels

FuelFS

Fuels used as feedstock in the Demand and Emissions Modules. Subset of Fuel set.

FuelOG

Fuels used in the oil and gas production sector. Subset of Fuel set.

GenCo

Generating companies simulated in Electricity Module

GNode

Natural Gas Transmission Module nodes

H2Tech

Types of Hydrogen Supply technologies

Hour

Representative hour in Electricity Module

Market

Placeholders for user-defined carbon markets (up to 220 market definitions possible)

Month

Months represented for electric dispatch

Nation

Nations represented in the model

NationX

From Nations used to represent source of flows for imports and exports

Node

Nodes represented in the Electricity Module

NodeX

From NodeX used in electricity dispatch

Offset

Types of offsets used for reduction curves

OGUnit

Oil and gas supply individual units represented in ENERGY 2100 

PCov

Types of pollution that can be covered in a carbon market

Plant

Electricity module plant types





Tech

Technology types within the residential, commercial, industrial, and transportation sectors

Year

Historical and forecast years represented in ENERGY 2100
